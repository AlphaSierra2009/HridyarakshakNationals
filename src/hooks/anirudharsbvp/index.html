About Us

Hridaya Rakshak is an initiative dedicated to heart health education and prevention. Our goal is to raise awareness, conduct health drives, and empower individuals with the knowledge to take charge of their cardiovascular well-being.

Our Mission

Our mission is to reduce the prevalence of heart disease through awareness programs, community screenings, and healthy lifestyle promotion.
